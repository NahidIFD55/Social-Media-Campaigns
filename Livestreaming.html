Livestreaming Safely

How to enjoy live broadcasting while protecting your privacy

Facebook Live Safety

Before You Go Live:

  • Check your privacy settings - choose who can see your stream
  • Review your surroundings - avoid showing personal details
  • Prepare moderators to help manage comments

During Your Stream:

  • Use comment filters to block offensive words
  • Pause if you need to address inappropriate behavior
  • Be mindful of sharing personal information

YouTube Live Safety

Before You Go Live:

  • Set your stream to "Unlisted" if you want limited visibility
  • Enable moderation tools in Creator Studio
  • Consider a slight delay to catch issues

During Your Stream:

  • Use the "Hold potentially inappropriate comments for review" setting
  • Assign trusted moderators
  • Save a copy of the stream for reference

TikTok Live Safety

Before You Go Live:

  • Set your account to private if you want to limit viewers
  • Use "Only Friends" or "Invite-only" options
  • Enable "Filter Keywords" in settings

During Your Stream:

  • Use the "Disable Comments" option if needed
  • Block users who violate community guidelines
  • Be aware of your background and surroundings

General Livestreaming Safety Tips

Privacy Protection

  • Avoid showing documents, mail, or anything with personal information
  • Be cautious about revealing your location (turn off location services)
  • Consider using a virtual background if streaming from home

Content Considerations

  • Have a clear plan for your stream content
  • Avoid spontaneous sharing that you might regret later
  • Remember that even "disappearing" streams can be recorded

Dealing with Harassment

  • Know how to quickly block and report users
  • Don't engage with trolls - mute or ban instead
  • Take breaks if the stream becomes stressful
  • Have an exit plan if the situation escalates

Parental Guidance

For parents of teens who livestream:

  • Discuss appropriate streaming content
  • Review privacy settings together
  • Encourage streaming in common areas of the home
  • Set time limits for streaming sessions

Emergency Steps

If a stream goes wrong:

  1. End the broadcast immediately
  2. Report any harassment to the platform
  3. Document the incident with screenshots
  4. Contact platform support if content needs removal
  5. Reach out to a trusted adult if needed
