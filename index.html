Stay Safe Online

Facebook — 3.05 billion users

Facebook was founded in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg and his college roommates. It's now the largest social media platform in the world.

Safety Tips:

  • Protect your password
  • Never share login information
  • Log out on shared devices
  • Be cautious with friend requests
  • Watch for malicious software

YouTube — 2.49 billion users

Launched in 2005, YouTube is now the second most visited website globally, offering video content on every imaginable topic.

Safety Features:

  • Block inappropriate comments
  • Filter specific words
  • Hold comments for review
  • Restrict live chat
  • Parental controls available

TikTok — 1.22 billion users

The Chinese app launched in 2016 has become a global phenomenon, especially popular with younger audiences.

Account Security:

  • Use unique passwords
  • Avoid common phrases
  • Combine letters, numbers, symbols
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Be mindful of shared content
