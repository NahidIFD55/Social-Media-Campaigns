About Our Campaigns

Our social media safety campaigns are designed to educate and empower teenagers to navigate the digital world safely. We focus on providing practical advice and resources to help young people make informed decisions online.

Key Objectives

  • Educate about online risks and how to avoid them
  • Promote digital literacy and critical thinking skills
  • Encourage positive online behavior and digital citizenship
  • Provide resources for dealing with cyberbullying
  • Help build resilience against online negativity

Our Approach

We believe in a balanced approach that doesn't demonize social media but teaches responsible use. Our campaigns are:

  • Evidence-based: Using the latest research in digital behavior
  • Peer-led: Engaging young people in creating content
  • Practical: Providing actionable advice, not just warnings
  • Positive: Focusing on opportunities as well as risks

Campaign Highlights

Our current initiatives include:

  • #ThinkBeforeYouPost - Promoting mindful sharing
  • Privacy Matters - Understanding data protection
  • Digital Detox Challenge - Healthy social media habits
  • Upstander Program - Standing up to cyberbullying
  • FactCheck - Spotting fake news online
